The buddy box function uses a wire between the two transmitters that is a standard 3.5 mm jack stereo or mono audio cable.||

When using the Buddy box feature, one switch must be defined as 'Buddy'. It's best if this is a spring-return switch. Connect two transmitters with a normal 3.5 mm jack audio cable to test, and if the Buddy function is not at the ideal switch position, re-calibrate the switches and/or reverse the Buddy switch sense.
Don't forget to select which channels are enabled for the Buddy. Any channels not selected will remain always under the control of the Master.||

After changing the buddy settings, the transmitter must be re-started.

The selected buddy channels are saved on a per model basis.||
