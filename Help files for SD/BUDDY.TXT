The buddy box function uses a wire between the two transmitters that is a standard 3.5 mm jack stereo audio cable.||

One of the edge switches, preferably with a spring return, should be setup as the Buddy switch.

Don't forget to select which channels are enabled for the Buddy. Any channels not selected will remain always under the control of the Master.||

After changing the buddy settings, the transmitter must re-boot.

The selected buddy channels are saved on a per model basis.||
