This screen shows the position of all channels, and allows the editing of a curves for each channel and bank.||
 
Press a button at the left or right to edit any of the 64 possible curves.||

(16 channels x 4 banks modes = 64.)||

Be sure to select intended bank and model before editing a curve.||

It's possible to copy a curve to all flight modes by means of the check box.||

The option a the bottom for 'Dual Rates' is a quick and easy way to setup several banks all at once, each as a proportion of a 'main' bank. This means you only need to set up the main bank manually.||

('Bank' is just another word for 'flight mode'.)
