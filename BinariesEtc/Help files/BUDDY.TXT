The buddy box function uses a wire between the two transmitters that is a standard 3.5 mm jack stereo audio cable with its two signal wires crossed over.

(Cross over the two that are not GND. This crossover can of course be in the connecting wire, OR in one of the transmitters, allowing an unaltered audio wire to be used.)||

The Trigger channel can be any (1-16), and is usually setup on a switch.||

'Buddy' has control control when the channel is at or close to 100%. |
'Master' gets control back when the channel is well below its maximum.||

Don't forget to select which channels are enabled for the Buddy. All channels not selected will remain under the control of the Master which can be a great help with early-stage beginners.||

The selected channels are saved on a per model basis.||
