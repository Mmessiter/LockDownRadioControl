Notes

  • SBUS: universal legacy. Inverted 100 kbaud 8E2. 16 ch.
  • CRSF: modern standard, ~250 Hz, used by Crossfire/ExpressLRS. 16 ch packed.
  • IBUS: FlySky / many budget gear. 115200 8N1. 14 ch.
  • PPM: classic single-pin pulse train. Useful for sim adapters (RX2SIM, Phoenix, etc). 8 ch.

Same pin (D6) for all four — the chip reconfigures it as either an inverted/non-inverted UART or as an RMT pulse generator at boot, depending on which protocol you select.
