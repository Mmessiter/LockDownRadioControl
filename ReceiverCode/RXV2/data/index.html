Welcome — let's set this receiver up

Give the receiver a unique name and (optionally) your home WiFi details. Filling both in here saves an extra reboot.

Receiver name (required)

Letters, digits and hyphens only. No spaces. Up to 30 characters.

Home WiFi (optional — leave blank to set later)

What happens next: the receiver reboots and reappears as <name>. If you provided WiFi details, it also joins your home network at <name>.local — switch your phone back to home WiFi and visit that address. If you didn't, reconnect to the <name> WiFi network and come back here to add them.
