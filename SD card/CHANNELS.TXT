This screen shows the position of all channels, and allows the editing of the curves for each channel and flight mode. Press a button at the left or right to edit any of the 64 curves. (16 channels x 4 flight modes = 64). Be sure to select the intended flight mode and model before editing a curve.

It's possible to save a curve to all four flight modes by means of a check box. This avoids your having to set identical settings several times.
