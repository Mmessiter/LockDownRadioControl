Four colours can be defined here. These change the appearance and visibility of the transmitter. These colours are 'Normal', 'Highlighted','Special' and 'Background'. Each colour is built individually, by varying its amounts of RED, GREEN, and BLUE. || FIRST select which colour to define, THEN slide the three
colours around until you like what you see. Press 'Defaults' to return all to default values. ||Avoid choosing hard-to-see colours, and in particlar be sure all colours contrast well against the background colour.
