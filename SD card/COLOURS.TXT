Four colours can be defined here. These can change the 
appearance and visibility of the transmitter.

These are 'Normal', 'Highlighted','Special' and 'Background'.

Each colour is built individually, just by varying its 
amounts of RED, GREEN, and BLUE.

FIRST select which colour to define, THEN slide the three
colours around until you like what you see.

Press 'Defaults' to return all to default values.
