This screen should usually be visible while flying a model because it shows the Bank, time, trims, motor timer and battery voltages. ||

The motor timer runs automaticaly when the model is NOT in Bank 4 in order to time the motor use. So it's recommended to setup the model such that the motor is always off in Bank 4.||

The timer pauses while in Bank 4 and is reset when the transmitter is turned on.||

The LED is Blue when the radio is not even trying to connect.|
The LED is RED when the radio is trying to connect.
The LED is GREEN when a connection exists.

When the LED is green, the 'Off' button is disabled to prevent accidental disconnection. If you really what to switch off when still connected, you can from the Options screen.

Press 'Setup' to change all settings.
