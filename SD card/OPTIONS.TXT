'QNH'means atmospheric pressure at sea level for
the current time and location. This is needed for 
more accurate altitude readings from the BMP280
barometer, assuming one was fitted. Aviation web 
sites will give this information for nearby airports.

Trim factor selects how much trim to apply.
Hours from GMT selects your time zone. This is needed
if GPS is connected as it will corrent the Real Time Clock
to GMT if zone is not known.
