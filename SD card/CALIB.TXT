It's *VERY* important to use this option before setting up and flying.|
The system needs to know the end points and centre points of the sticks' and the knobs' potentiometers (or switches).||

Only channels 1-8 need to be calibrated.|
When calibrating, it's recommended to use a 'Deleted' model memory.| 
So set that first. Press the button ONCE, then wiggle the sticks and move the knobs several times to all their extreme positions, then press the button a second time, then put all sticks etc. to their exact centre positions.|
Then press it a third time to record these centre positions.|
Don't press the button a forth time unless you  want to calibrate all over again. Press OK to exit.
