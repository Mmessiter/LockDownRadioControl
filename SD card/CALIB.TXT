It's very important to use this option before setting up and flying.||

The system needs to know the end points and centre points of the 
sticks' and the knobs' potentiometers. Only channels 1-8 need to be
calibrated.||

Press the button ONCE, then wiggle the sticks and move the knobs 
several times to all their extreme positions, then press the 
button ONCE again, then put all to their exact centre positions. 
Then press it one last time to record centres. ||

Then press OK.
