To move a point up or down, touch the screen above or below the point. ||

That point will move close to where you touched. For finer adjustment, use the buttons at the bottom left.||

Three type of interpolation are supported:||

1. Straight: 4 straight lines joining 5 points.|
2. Smooth: Smooth curve between the same 5 points.|
3. Exponential: 3 points joined by 2 exponential curves with expo values between 0% ( = straight) and 100%.
